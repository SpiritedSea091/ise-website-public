Indian School of Excellence

Indian School of Excellence is a school in Bangalore East. Established in the year 2017, the school was built with the aim of achieving well-rounded development in its students. Towards this goal, Indian School of Excellence has a curriculum that is all-encompassing with respect to the types of classes students are exposed to in the school. The vision of the school is to promote a sense of Indian culture while keeping students ready for global endeavors. The mission of the school is to evolve a natural sense of freedom and passion in students. Indian School of Excellence has been built and developed to lead your child into the twenty-first century with a competitive edge.

Curriculum

The curriculum at Indian School of Excellence is a blend of multiple curriculums. It is centered around the CBSE curriculum at its base but uses the best features of other curriculums like the ICSE and IGCSE to supplement the CBSE curriculum in areas where the school believes it is lacking. Chief among these areas are English and Computer Science. English language in the school uses IGCSE textbooks and its prescribed curriculum which is the highest quality in the school's opinion. The computer science subject supplements the CBSE curriculum with additional classes which are expected to give students a competitive edge in a skill which is indispensable in the past few decades.

Campus

Indian School of Excellence has a campus that is the envy of many! The school boasts a lavish 6-acre campus which has been designed by a team of highly acclaimed architects. The school is landscaped with beautiful trees and shrubs to support young students longing for natural environments. The trees planted are also supported by the students and parents of the school. The first batch of parents was involved in planting a row of trees in the school. The school has a large sports field where sports like football, cricket, basketball, skating, tennis and many more are practiced by students. The school also has no dearth of indoor sports and activities like table tennis, carrom board, chess and more.

Teaching Methods 

The teaching method at ISE is based on the 5E model which was developed internally by the brightest of the school’s teaching talent. The 5E model stands for Explore, Engage, Explain, Educate and Evaluate. The stress of learning is not on rote learning but on active intuitive understanding based on real-life practice and exploration. The classes for any subject are taught quite unlike most conventional classes; this school uses innovative methods to allow students to “discover” concepts for themselves while supporting their exploration and inquisitiveness. 

Location Details

Indian School of Excellence is located in Kithaganur, Bangalore East. It is very frequently chosen by parents looking for CBSE schools in Ramamurthy Nagar Bangalore. It is located in a great locality removed from the noise and pollution of the highly trafficked environment near Bangalore’s Outer Ring Road. It is still highly accessible from multiple key locations of the city like KR Puram and Old Madras Road.
