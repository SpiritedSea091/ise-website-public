Part 2 - How we can help

Find out how Indian School of Excellence can help your child in their growth and development.

Learning by doing, rather than just hearing.

Although school prepares a child to solve math and science problems 🧪 , the problems they will have to face after their years in school are much more varied. 

A question to you the parent - do you remember using calculus after you finished school and college? When was the last time you had to balance a chemical equation in your everyday life?

Very likely you currently use skills that you learned after your time in school - perhaps in college or at work. 💼

A time came for you to realise that you need skills that you did not learn in school. And how were you supposed to gain those skills? Call up your teacher 🧑🏫  from fifteen years ago and ask them what to study, and what was “important for the test”?

No. You probably learned by doing. You experimented 🔬  , created a demo project, did some research independently or reached out to a colleague for help.

This is how learning is usually done in the real world 🌏 . You are rarely told exactly what to do all the time. You need to be able to figure things out for yourself, learn by yourself. You need to become a hands-on learner.

The rapid rate of progress in the realm of technology📱 compounds this fact. Things you learn now will quickly become obsolete - maybe in a few years, maybe less. Such an environment compels one to constantly learn and adapt.  Again you need to be a hands-on learner.

This was identified as a major concern by the founders of Indian School of Excellence 🏫 . For this reason, the school introduces experiential learning into the classroom starting at very young ages. Children are given the opportunity to take part in group activities, independent research assignments and exploratory projects.

These initiatives teach children how to learn independently. As a result, more than learning any particular concept, children learn how to learn. 💯  

This enables them to learn anything they need to in the future. So whether it is blockchain, AI, robotics or some other skill that is in-demand, your child will be able to quickly learn and adapt. They will have mastered the skill of hands-on learning. 💪
