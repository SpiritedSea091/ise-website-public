Director's Message

Indian School of Excellence was started in the year 2017 with a group of eminent educationists who got together to have an institution which produce students who are life ready and not just job ready. We have brought in practices where students are taught to be self learners. Life skills like food science, cooking, sewing, carpentry, electrical troubleshooting are part of the learning process at ISE. The system is developed in such a way that they learn how to comprehend, communicate and articulate whatever they have learned. We have integrated the best of Cambridge, ICSE and CBSE curriculum upto grade 8. Adopting the best from different curriculums gives our students a solid foundation for their future.
